Anatomy Collection & Anatomy Studies

"Hand of God" is a powerful and thought-provoking artistic rendition that portrays a hand reaching out, evoking a sense of divine intervention and transcendence. The artwork captures the hand with meticulous attention to detail, emphasizing its strength, grace, and ethereal presence. The hand is imbued with a sense of authority, representing a guiding force or higher power extending its influence into the earthly realm.

"Heart" is a captivating pixel artistic rendition that portrays an anatomically correct representation of the human heart. Created with meticulous attention to detail, this artwork celebrates the intricate beauty and significance of this vital organ. Each pixel is purposefully placed to capture the complexity and elegance of the heart's structure, showcasing the artist's skill and precision.

Using a chromatic pallete whose colors are somewhat blown out these sketches of arms exude character where none can be seen, using literal body language each limb shows its own sense of personality, emotion, character, etc.

This hand was carved with only the most detail emulating a hand of a elderly man the scars of time are evident among the details, showing noise thrown throughout the piece, the over saturated shadows and the broken shading resembling dying skin.

These anatomically incorrect or imperfect skulls are made with no beauty, by combining linework, sketches, and rendering all onto one layer we have an almagamation of a work process without seeing the process.
