Welcome to Srnahmeer's Art Portfolio

A short introduction

Hello! My name is Shahmeer and I go under the name Srnahmeer I've been an avid pixel artist for 3 years, after dabbling in multiple mediums which include SFX, 3D modeling, 2D art, conceptual art, architecture, and many more I've found my calling to pixel art. My speciality are dynamic poses and scenes. Unless you'd like to learn more I'd recommend checking out the rest of the page. I've done dozens of commisions and hopefully as I learn how to develop you can see these assets being used in a future game.

Skills

Dynamic Poses

I specialize in only the most dynamic of poses created from models, everyday people, and just any plain image, and stylize it in my own to make it burst with character.

Pop-Art

Blending with Dynmamic Poses I also make pop-art, limiting my pallete and relying on cross hatching techniques, what I call "beautiful noise" and many other techniques, I make even a 6 color pallete feel like 600.

Pallete Creation

The most important part of a piece is where the pixels are placed, but what makes them pretty are the colors they're placed with; I have the ability to make dynamic palletes coorelating with the piece to spark emotion within the viewer.

Game Developer Capabilities

In lieu of my artistic capabilities, I have the ability to combine them with my current knowledge of game developing; I soon hope to release the beta of a game I have been developing for months.
